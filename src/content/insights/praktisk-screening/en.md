---
title: "Practical screening: better signal, less noise"
category: "Screening"
excerpt: "Screening isn't about finding more hits — it's about finding the right ones. Here's how to increase precision."
date: "2025-10-04"
---

PEP and sanctions screening is a regulatory requirement, but in practice many tools generate enormous volumes of false positives. This costs time and creates fatigue among case handlers.

Better screening is about combining data sources intelligently, using fuzzy matching wisely, and giving the handler context directly in the interface. The goal is that every hit requiring manual review is truly relevant.

Modern platforms can also offer ongoing monitoring, so you don't just screen at onboarding but catch changes — like a new PEP classification or a sanctions listing — in real time.
